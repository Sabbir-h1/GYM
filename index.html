Wednesday & Sunday — Extra Cardio Day (no weights)

On these 2 days, skip the weights and do double cardio instead: Cross trainer fat burner 20 min → rest 5 min → Treadmill incline walk 30 min → Bodyweight exercises (jumping jacks 3×50, high knees 3×30s, burpees 3×10). Total ~400–500 extra calories. This is how you accelerate the fat loss.

Weekly Strength Split

Simple.
Effective.
Beginner-Safe.

All exercises are machine or dumbbell based — no barbell, no deadlift, no bench press. Light-to-medium weight, slow and controlled reps. Feel the muscle, don't just move the weight.

💪 Chest
Weights
  • Machine Chest Press
    Sit upright, push straight. Don't arch back.
    3 × 12
  • Incline Dumbbell Press
    45° incline, squeeze chest at top
    3 × 12
  • Cable Chest Fly
    Wide arc, slight bend in elbows
    3 × 15
  • Push-ups (wide grip)
    Do as many as you can — even 5 is fine
    2 × max
✓ Rest 60–75 sec. Light weight, feel the chest squeeze.
🦾 Triceps + Abs
Arms & Core
  • Cable Tricep Pushdown
    Elbows stay pinned to your sides
    3 × 15
  • Overhead Dumbbell Extension
    Both hands on one dumbbell, lower slowly
    3 × 12
  • Plank
    Straight line head to toe, breathe
    3 × 30s
  • Crunches
    Exhale at top, don't pull neck
    3 × 20
✓ Monday focus: Chest gets priority. Triceps are bonus today.
🏋️ Back
Weights
  • Lat Pulldown (wide grip)
    Pull to upper chest, lean back slightly
    3 × 12
  • Seated Cable Row
    Squeeze shoulder blades together at the end
    3 × 12
  • Dumbbell Single-Arm Row
    Knee on bench, pull elbow to ceiling
    3 × 12 each
  • Face Pulls (cable rope)
    Pull to forehead level, elbows high
    3 × 15
✓ Think "pull with elbows not hands" — that's how you feel your back.
💪 Biceps + Abs
Arms & Core
  • Dumbbell Bicep Curl
    Twist wrist outward at top. Don't swing.
    3 × 15
  • Hammer Curl
    Neutral grip (thumbs up), controlled
    3 × 12
  • Lying Leg Raises
    Slow and controlled, lower legs slowly
    3 × 15
  • Mountain Climbers
    Drive knees fast to chest
    3 × 30s
✓ Biceps grow slow — be patient. Back is what matters most today.
