Form Over Weight

The Exercise Progression Ladders

Start at the specific tier matching your current execution form. Advance to the lower tiers only when you can confidently master target rep schemes with clean control.

Push-Up Progression
  • 1. Wall Push-ups
  • ↓ Incline (Table)
  • ↓ Incline (Chair)
  • ↓ Knee Push-ups
  • ↓ Negative Push-ups
  • ↓ Full Push-ups
  • ↓ Diamond / Decline
Squat Progression
  • 1. Chair Squat
  • ↓ Bodyweight Squat
  • ↓ Pause Squat
  • ↓ Split Squat
  • ↓ Reverse Lunge
  • ↓ Bulgarian Split Squat
  • ↓ Jump Squat
Core Progression
  • 1. Dead Bug
  • ↓ Plank (30 sec)
  • ↓ Plank (60 sec)
  • ↓ Side Plank
  • ↓ Leg Raises
  • ↓ Hollow Hold
  • ↓ V-ups
Glutes Progression
  • 1. Glute Bridge
  • ↓ Single-leg Bridge

  • ↓ Single-leg Hip Thrust
